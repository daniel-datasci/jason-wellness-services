Hi there! I’m Favour Okpala.

I'm an experienced Clinical Psychologist and Art Therapist with over 7 years of experience in the mental health sector. I believe that change is a process and that meaningful change requires effort and commitment. I view therapy as tool that can help you gain insight into your thoughts, feelings, and behaviours while empowering you with the tools necessary to make positive changes.
I hold a Master's degree in Clinical Psychology from the University of Lagos. My areas of expertise include but are not limited to:

  • Trauma Recovery
  • Stress management
  • Conflict Resolution
  • Emotional Intelligence
  • Communication Issues
  • Art Therapy

I provide services to children, adolescents and adults and I utilize a variety of evidence-based interventions. I believe that every individual has the potential to lead a fulfilling life, so it is my goal and priority to create a safe and collaborative therapeutic environment that allows for you to identify and reach your goals.

Couples Therapy

At Jason Wellness Services, we acknowledge that nurturing emotional connections within relationships is fundamental to their strength and resilience. Our couples therapy sessions offer a unique avenue for partners to explore and express emotions beyond words. Using various artistic mediums as catalysts, such as painting, drawing, and collaborative creations, we guide couples in uncovering insights into their emotional dynamics.

Verbal communication might pose challenges in expressing intricate feelings within a relationship. Therefore, our approach embraces non-verbal expression through art, fostering a deeper understanding and empathy between partners.

Each couples therapy session is tailored to address the unique needs and dynamics of the relationship. Our skilled therapists facilitate a safe and empathetic environment, encouraging open exploration of emotions and experiences.

Through this creative process, couples embark on a journey of understanding, learning effective communication strategies, fostering mutual respect, and nurturing a stronger emotional bond. Our aim is to support couples in developing resilience, enhancing intimacy, and fostering a thriving, harmonious relationship.
